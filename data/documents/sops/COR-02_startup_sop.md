# SOP — Safe Start-up Procedure: Coke Oven Pushing Ram (COR-02)

**Applies to:** COR-02 | **Area:** Coke Making | **Criticality:** High

## Purpose
This SOP defines the approved steps for the safe start-up procedure of the Coke Oven Pushing Ram to ensure personnel safety, equipment integrity, and process continuity.

## Pre-requisites
- Valid work permit and PPE (helmet, gloves, safety shoes, hearing protection).
- Communication established with control room.
- Relevant isolation points identified.

## Procedure
1. Confirm all maintenance locks/tags removed and area clear.
2. Verify lubrication levels and cooling media availability.
3. Energise auxiliaries (lube, cooling) and confirm healthy readings.
4. Start main drive at minimum load.
5. Ramp to operating point while watching Section 2 parameters.
6. Hand over to operations once stable.

## Records
Log all actions, readings, and consumed spares in the digital maintenance logbook.
