# SOP — Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) Procedure: Cooling Water Pump (CWP-04)

**Applies to:** CWP-04 | **Area:** Utilities | **Criticality:** Medium

## Purpose
This SOP defines the approved steps for the lockout-tagout (loto) procedure of the Cooling Water Pump to ensure personnel safety, equipment integrity, and process continuity.

## Pre-requisites
- Valid work permit and PPE (helmet, gloves, safety shoes, hearing protection).
- Communication established with control room.
- Relevant isolation points identified.

## Procedure
1. Notify control room and obtain permit-to-work.
2. Identify all energy sources (electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic, thermal).
3. Switch off and isolate the main drive at the local isolator.
4. Apply personal locks and tags at each isolation point.
5. Dissipate stored energy (bleed hydraulic/pneumatic pressure, allow thermal cool-down).
6. Verify zero-energy state using approved test method.
7. Proceed with maintenance only after verification.

## Records
Log all actions, readings, and consumed spares in the digital maintenance logbook.
