# SOP — Fault Response Procedure: Sinter Plant ID Fan (SP-IDF-03)

**Applies to:** SP-IDF-03 | **Area:** Sinter Making | **Criticality:** High

## Purpose
This SOP defines the approved steps for the fault response procedure of the Sinter Plant ID Fan to ensure personnel safety, equipment integrity, and process continuity.

## Pre-requisites
- Valid work permit and PPE (helmet, gloves, safety shoes, hearing protection).
- Communication established with control room.
- Relevant isolation points identified.

## Procedure
1. On alarm, record the active parameter excursion against Section 2 limits.
2. Reduce load / transfer duty to standby unit if available.
3. Inspect for the most probable fault mode (e.g., Impeller dust build-up / imbalance).
4. Isolate per the LOTO SOP before any physical intervention.
5. Diagnose using the troubleshooting guide in the equipment manual.
6. Replace confirmed worn spares; record part numbers consumed.
7. Re-commission per the start-up SOP and monitor trend for 24 h.

## Records
Log all actions, readings, and consumed spares in the digital maintenance logbook.
