# SOP — Controlled Shutdown Procedure: Sinter Plant ID Fan (SP-IDF-02)

**Applies to:** SP-IDF-02 | **Area:** Sinter Making | **Criticality:** High

## Purpose
This SOP defines the approved steps for the controlled shutdown procedure of the Sinter Plant ID Fan to ensure personnel safety, equipment integrity, and process continuity.

## Pre-requisites
- Valid work permit and PPE (helmet, gloves, safety shoes, hearing protection).
- Communication established with control room.
- Relevant isolation points identified.

## Procedure
1. Obtain control-room clearance to stop.
2. Reduce load progressively to minimum.
3. Stop the main drive.
4. Keep auxiliaries running for cool-down as specified.
5. Isolate if maintenance is to follow (see LOTO SOP).

## Records
Log all actions, readings, and consumed spares in the digital maintenance logbook.
