## 3. Common Fault Modes
### 3.1 Impeller dust build-up / imbalance
Dust accretion on impeller raises vibration and unbalance.

**Typical root causes:** Inadequate gas cleaning, High dust load, Missed cleaning cycle.

### 3.2 Impeller erosion
Abrasive dust thins blades, shifting balance over time.

**Typical root causes:** High particulate, Long service hours, Wrong blade material.

### 3.3 Bearing failure
Bearing temperature and vibration rise sharply before seizure.

**Typical root causes:** Lube starvation, Water ingress, Overheating.
