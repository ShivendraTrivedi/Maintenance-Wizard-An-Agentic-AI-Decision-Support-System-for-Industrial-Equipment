# Failure Analysis Report — CWP-08 — FR-20241009-002

**Asset:** CWP-08 (Cooling Water Pump)  
**Area:** Utilities  
**Date of failure:** 2024-10-09  
**Downtime:** 24 hours  
**Fault mode:** Mechanical seal leak  

## Event Description
Operations reported an abnormal condition on CWP-08. Seal failure causes leakage and bearing contamination. The parameter 'vibration' deviated from its normal band (0.8–4.5 mm/s) prior to the trip.

## Root Cause
Investigation concluded the primary root cause was **dry running**, leading to mechanical seal leak.

## Corrective Action Taken
- Isolated the asset per LOTO SOP.
- Replaced affected component (Pump impeller).
- Restored normal operating parameters and monitored for 24 hours.

## Preventive Recommendation
- Tighten inspection interval for the affected sub-assembly.
- Add 'vibration' to the early-warning watch list with alert at 4 mm/s.
- Ensure spare 'Bearing set' is held in stock given lead time.
